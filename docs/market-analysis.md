# Prophet 21 Consulting Market Analysis

**Date:** February 12, 2026
**Prepared for:** Lumina Erp Positioning Strategy

## Executive Summary

The Prophet 21 consulting market consists of established firms offering traditional implementation, customization, and support services. Pricing ranges from $200/hour for project work to $40-100K+ for implementations. The market shows gaps in performance optimization, modern development practices, and transparent pricing—areas where Lumina Erp can differentiate.
